Hawaii

Paradise Awaits.

Black, red, green and more! These are the sand colors of some of Hawaii's beaches. Drive the scenic "Road to Hana" on Maui, and discover both black and red sand beaches. If you like homemade banana bread, be sure to take a break at Aunt Sandy's Banana Bread stand. Half way to Hana turn left off the highway. There are some great places to take pictures along that road - think blue waters and palm trees.

There are 13 Wold Climate regions in the world and Hawaii has 11 of them. Most of them can be found on the Big Island alone.

Tours

Explore In Comfort

Interested in venturing to a place far away, but don't feel like renting a car or navigating the transit system? Tours can be a great way to start. No more wasted time looking at maps and planning out your next day in a hotel room. It's all taken care of for you.

There are many tour operators to fit your style of travel. Whether you prefer small groups, age specific, or themed, there is something for you. Globus family of brands has escorted tours in 70 countries across six continents. They also have river cruise options available through Avalon Waterways . Imagine floating down the Danube, Amazon, or Nile and wake up each morning to explore a new city or village - only unpacking your bag once!
