Hands Up! Hands down.

Remember the days when you could stroll through security without your arms in a "Stick 'em up!" style?

When you could leave your shoes and jacket on and walk through the detectors with your dignity still intact? Awe, those days are back! Well, kind of, as long as you pay off the Federal Government. To the tune of $85 you may have the privilege to enter a special line at airport security called TSA Precheck. Yes, you really can keep your belts on! And, no need to unpack your laptop or 3-1-1 liquids.

If you like this option and you travel internationally, you may want to ante up $15 more and upgrade to Global Entry. This program expedites your return to the U.S. with special lines at passport control, plus no filling out the blue declaration card. TSA Precheck is automatically included with enrollment. Yippee!

Both programs are good for 5 years. If you have a fancy credit card, such as an American Express Platinum card, or a Citi Prestige, your enrollment fee for either program is reimbursed.
